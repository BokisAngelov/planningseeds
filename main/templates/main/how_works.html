How it works

Registration

Create a new account or log in to an existing one. Choose between Customer or Producer.

Create an account

Customer

STEP 1: Request Offers

Customers can find and request quotes for specific products. Each request includes details such as product specifications, quantities etc.

STEP 2: Place Orders

Customers can place orders based on received offers. No financial transactions will be processed through the platform. A dashboard with the history of orders will be visible.

View products

Producer

STEP 1: Send Offers to Customers

Producers can review requests and send detailed offers to customers. Offers include pricing, delivery timelines, and other relevant terms. A dashboard with the history of sent offers will be visible.

STEP 2: Use Template to Send Invoices

Producers will generate invoices using predefined templates.
